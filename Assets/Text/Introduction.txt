The year is 2134; man has discovered hyper space travel. 
Due to extreme overpopulation of our own solar system, NASA has deployed several teams to search for other deep space regions to claim as our own. 
Upon investigation of nearby planetary systems, Stella Kern and her team of space explorers discover the human habitable planetary system, Xylo, not too far from their location. Thus, they embark on their journey. 
However, during their travel to Xylo, something goes horribly wrong. 
Their spacecraft, the Ancora, crash lands on an unfamiliar planet. 
Stella wakes up alone and confused. 
There is no time to panic, she must find her crewmates and figure out a way to survive.
